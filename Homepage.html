JVM'S (Jnan Vikas Mandal's) NEW ENGLISH SCHOOL & PADMASHRI DR. R. T. DOSHI JR. COLLEGE OF COMMERCE & SCIENCE (Linguist Minority- Kannada),KALWA was established in 1974. We provide education in following age-groups.

  1. Pre-Primary
  2. Primary
  3. Secondary
  4. Jr. College

Our institution was started in 1974 with the objective of imparting eduation to the residants of Kalwa and surrounding areas. K.G. classes were started in a small room with few students. With persistent efforts, unparalleled dedication gradually developed. The school became fullfledged in 1986. the first batch of students appeared for S.S.C. Board Examination which brought an insiring result of 92%. Our institution completed its silver jubilee in September 1999 and Ruby jubilee in September 2014. It's a proud movement that we started Degree College in faculties of Arts, Science & Commerce from June 2002.
